Peltier Lake Drawdown
This static map was created to showcase the estimated change in water elevation for a lake drawdown in the Rice Creek Watershed District. The goal of the drawdown was to combat an invasive species and improve water quality. The data was derived using bathymetric data and raster analsyis.
